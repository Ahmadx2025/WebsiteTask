About JordanChalk
Our Story
Jordan Chalk is a family business that was established in 2002. It is specialized in providing and manufacturing high quality products through using unique raw materials comprised of calcium carbonate found in huge quantities across Jordan. This has enabled us to export 98% of our product to over 100 countries, of which are numerous well-known brands.

Committed to Sustainability & Social Responsibility
Jordan Chalk’s CSR & Sustainability
From a humble beginning in a small room to becoming the world’s second-largest chalk manufacturer and the leading one in the Middle East, Jordan Chalk is dedicated to empowering communities and promoting sustainability. Our operations run on 100% renewable and eco-friendly energy with 0% harmful emissions, taking a major step toward a greener future.
