Committed to Sustainability & Social Responsibility

Committed to a Greener and Fairer Future

From a humble beginning in a small room to becoming the world’s second-largest chalk manufacturer and the leading one in the Middle East, Jordan Chalk is dedicated to empowering communities and promoting sustainability. Our operations run on 100% renewable and eco-friendly energy with 0% harmful emissions, taking a major step toward a greener future. We believe that sustainability is not only about reducing environmental impact but also about investing in people and places.

Empowering Communities & Women in the Workforce

As part of our commitment to social responsibility, we take pride in fostering inclusivity and economic empowerment by maintaining a high percentage of female employees in our workforce. Our people are at the heart of what we do, and we ensure equal opportunities for all. Moreover, by anchoring our operations in Karak, one of Jordan’s less privileged cities, we actively support local economic growth and job creation, helping uplift the community and contribute to long-term sustainable progress.

Our Ethical Standards & Workplace Policies

At Jordan Chalk, we are guided by a robust ethical framework that promotes fairness, safety, and transparency across our operations. Our policies are designed to protect the rights of every individual and uphold the highest standards of workplace integrity. We are proud to share the following policies, which reflect our commitment to responsible and sustainable business practices:

Code of Conduct & Workplace Policies:

  • Decent Working Hours policy
  • Non-Gender Discrimination policy
  • Fair Remuneration policy
  • Harassment policy
  • Freedom of Associating Labor Unions policy
  • Reporting of Illegal or Unethical Behavior policy
  • Child Labor Policy
  • Protection and Proper Use of Company Assets
  • Forced labor policy
  • Employee Relations Policy
  • Safety Policy
  • Non-discrimination policy

These policies are more than internal documents — they are a reflection of how we do business and treat people. They ensure that every person at Jordan Chalk works in a safe, respectful, and equitable environment.

A Continuous Commitment

Our journey is far from over. Jordan Chalk remains committed to expanding its environmental initiatives, strengthening community ties, and constantly improving working conditions. We strive to be a model of sustainable industrial success — one that values people, planet, and progress.
